== Description ==

Don't have a Wordpress site? It's also available as a Javascript Bundle or API. Fill out [this form](https://forms.gle/vkESeLjEAV5RWq1Y7) for more info.

Take your movie and TV recommendations to the next level by showing your readers where every title is available to stream online! The Reelgood Publisher's Widget provides up-to-the-second information on where your audience can immediately start watching any series or movie with just one click.

Transform your posts from simple watchlists to reader-friendly "where to watch" lists!

The Reelgood Publisher's Widget supports over 130,000 titles on over 300 streaming platforms which include popular subscription-based services like Netflix, Disney+, Hulu, Prime Video and HBO, rent/buy options such as Google Play, Vudu, iTunes and Amazon, and even free services like Sony Crackle, IMDb TV, Kanopy, and more. 

Our on-page widget is mobile friendly, customizable, works with your existing affiliate codes to help you earn partner revenue, and automatically updates as content availability changes. Thanks to server-side rendering, the widget boasts ultra-fast load times and is fully visible to search bots which translates to increased traffic to your website from Google, Bing and others.

= How to Add Where to Watch Info =
Let's say you've created a blog post for the 10 best animated movies of all time. Once you're done writing, you're ready to add widgets that show readers where to watch each of the movies you recommended.

Assuming the plugin has been installed and you've added your API key, all that's left to do is to follow the steps below:

1. Click on the "Add Where to Watch" button above the Wordpress editor
2. Click on the "Add Where to Watch" button above the Wordpress editor
3. Select the right title from the search results
4. Click "Add"

That's it! The widget will appear in your post editor just as it will on your site once published. If you want to change the way it looks, just click "edit" and adjust the settings without ever leaving the Wordpress post editor!

== Installation ==

= Automatic Installation =

With automatic installation, WordPress handles everything itself so you don't have to worry about anything. To do an automatic install of Reelgood's Publisher's Widget, just log in to your WordPress dashboard, navigate to the Plugins menu, and click Add New.

In the search field, type "Reelgood" and hit Enter. Once you've found our widget, you can install it right away by clicking "Install Now". Alternatively, you can also look at more details about the latest release such as community reviews and ratings if you click on "More Details".

= Once activated =

1. On the Wordpress Navigation Bar, scroll down until you find "Reelgood" and click on it
2. This will open the Account Settings screen where you can set your own personal API key
3. Change the widget's behavior and styling to match your website's design via the "Default Styling" tab
4. Email [publishers@reelgood.com](mailto:publishers@reelgood.com) to add any relevant affiliate tracking to your API key

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

 = How do I add my affiliate codes? =

Simple, just email us at [publishers@reelgood.com](mailto:publishers@reelgood.com) and tell us what networks you are on and the relevant tracking codes. We store them on our database along with your API key. Whenever someone clicks a link from your site, we build the link along with your tracking information so you can get paid out on any relevant transactions

= Why does this cost money? =

Keeping track of all these shows and movies is no small task. We maintain hundreds of services and match all their libraries to our constantly updating database. We use machine learning along with our curation team to make sure everything is matched correctly and that we maintain the most timely, accurate data in our industry.
